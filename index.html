ABOUT

I’m Simon Urrea, owner of Keyless Solutions LLC, providing unparalleled automotive locksmith service in the Charlotte area. As a mobile service, we offer efficient services at your convenience with amazing communication at prices that will beat the dealers’. Our services include unlocking vehicles, lost keys, duplicate keys, key and fob programming, key shell replacements, and key cutting services for the American car market. I’m currently unable to take on European models at this time.

With a decade of automotive locksmithing experience, I created Keyless Solutions LLC because I believe dealer quality services should be accessible to everyone no matter where they are without the price tag. At the heart of Keyless Solutions LLC is customer service, putting my clients and their needs first. I believe in providing an exceptional experience every time. This company started out of the trunk of my Toyota Camry; through hard work, dedication to excellence, and word of mouth, Keyless Solutions has grown into a thriving business with a passion for helping as many people as possible. I look forward to helping you get back on the road quickly and safely.
